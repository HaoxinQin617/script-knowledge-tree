# Hermes Script Studio illustration profile

- One illustration per spoken script.
- Use a 16:10 landscape canvas for cards and reading-page covers.
- Use translucent liquid-glass objects, milky white space, mist violet, ice blue,
  mint, blush pink, and restrained butter yellow.
- Prefer symbolic objects and spatial relationships over literal metaphors.
- Do not render words, logos, watermarks, interface screenshots, or people.
- Keep the center subject clear and leave calm negative space around it.
